✦ What is NexHub?

NexHub is a curated web directory built and maintained by Nexus, a developer and creator from South Africa. It started as a personal bookmarks page and grew into a full-blown searchable hub of 288+ sites organised into 23 categories — from AI tools and dev resources to gaming, anime, music, and finance.

The goal is simple: cut through the noise. Every site listed here has been personally visited and verified. No ads, no sponsored placements, no algorithmic filler — just genuine tools worth your time.
